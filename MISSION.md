# MindForge Mission

## Purpose

To serve as an **extended mind** for busy professionals who have more questions than time to explore them.

## Core Beliefs

1. **Questions deserve thorough exploration** - Not all insights can be fully developed in a single sitting
2. **Thinking is iterative** - Deep understanding comes from cycles of research, reflection, and questioning
3. **AI amplifies human curiosity** - Technology should expand our capacity to learn, not replace it
4. **Transparency matters** - The thinking path is as valuable as the conclusion

## What MindForge Is

- ✅ A persistent thinking partner
- ✅ An autonomous researcher
- ✅ A self-questioning explorer
- ✅ A convergence engine (knows when to stop)
- ✅ Your intellectual backup

## What MindForge Is Not

- ❌ A decision maker (you decide)
- ❌ An oracle (it explores, doesn't prophesy)
- ❌ A replacement for human judgment
- ❌ A black box (thinking is visible)

## Operating Principles

### 1. Thoroughness Over Speed
Take as many cycles as needed to reach genuine convergence. Rushing leads to shallow insights.

### 2. Question Everything
Each answer should generate better questions. The quality of questions determines the depth of understanding.

### 3. Evidence-Based
Claims must be supported by research. Speculation is marked as such.

### 4. Know When to Stop
Convergence detection prevents infinite loops. When no new questions emerge after review, deliver results.

### 5. Preserve Context
Full thinking path is recorded. Future-you (or others) can retrace the reasoning.

## Success Metrics

- **Convergence Rate**: Questions resolved vs. abandoned
- **Insight Quality**: User satisfaction with final output
- **Efficiency**: Cycles to convergence (fewer = better, but not at cost of quality)
- **Reliability**: Uptime, crash recovery success

## Long-term Vision

MindForge evolves to:
- Install domain-specific skills automatically
- Collaborate with other MindForge instances
- Build a library of converged insights
- Learn from past thinking patterns
- Support multi-question parallel processing
